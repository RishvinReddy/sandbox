My Journey

Technology has always been more than just code to me—it's a tool to solve real-world problems. My journey began with a curiosity for how things work, leading me to dive deep into Embedded Systems and IoT prototypes using Arduino and ESP microcontrollers.

As I scaled my projects, I realized that connectivity brings vulnerability. This drove my passion for Cyber Security, mastering encryption standards (AES/SHA) and secure authentication flows. Today, I bridge the physical and digital worlds by integrating hardware sensors with secure, decentralized Blockchain ledgers.

I don't just build software; I engineer reliable, secure, and user-centric ecosystems—from the firmware up to the cloud.
